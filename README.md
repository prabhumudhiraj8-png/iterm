#Customer, Product, and Profitability Performance Analysis in Supply Chain Operations   
Subject: APL Logistics — Project Summary: Customer, Product & Profitability Analysis

This project analyzes profitability across customers, products, and markets for APL Logistics (KWE Group), shifting focus from operational speed to commercial intelligence.
It uncovers which customers, product categories, and global regions truly generate value — separating high-margin accounts from loss-making ones.
Discount-driven margin erosion is a key diagnostic focus, identifying thresholds where discounts silently destroy profit despite healthy revenue numbers.
The deliverables include an interactive Streamlit dashboard, an EDA research paper, and an executive summary — all built on a rich supply chain order dataset.
The ultimate goal is to equip APL Logistics leadership with the clarity to make pricing, customer, and category decisions that are profit-driven, not just revenue-driven.
