# candy

Factory-to-Customer Shipping Route Efficiency Analysis for Nassau Candy Distributor
Nassau Candy, a prominent confectionery distributor, operates a complex supply chain that begins at manufacturing facilities and ends at retail customers across various regions. A shipping route efficiency analysis for such a distributor examines how effectively products move through each stage of the logistics pipeline — from factory floors to distribution centers, and ultimately to end customers — with the goal of minimizing cost, time, and waste while maximizing delivery reliability.
